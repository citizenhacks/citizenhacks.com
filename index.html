About
Youth play an essential role in defining our digital future. That’s why we created CitizenHacks, a student-run hackathon that encourages the next generation of innovators and users to actively participate in building a digital world that works for everyone.
CitizenHacks is a platform for university and high school students to collaborate with educators, businesses, nonprofits, and governmental organizations in exploring the challenges and potential of creating technology that benefits society.
